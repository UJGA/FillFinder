Adderall Information

About Adderall

Adderall is a prescription medication used to treat attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) and narcolepsy. It contains a combination of amphetamine and dextroamphetamine, which are central nervous system stimulants.

The Current Shortage

Since October 2022, there has been an ongoing shortage of Adderall in the United States. This shortage has affected many patients who rely on the medication for their daily functioning. The FDA has acknowledged the shortage and is working with manufacturers to address the issue.

Important Reminders
